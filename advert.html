What is Adwords?

Adwords is a form of pay-per-click advertising in which you create an ad targeted at a specific demographic or set of keywords. Your cost is determined by the popularity of your keywords and how much you bid on them. You only pay when someone clicks on your ad. This is why it is called "pay-per-click".

Why use Adwords?

Setting up an Adwords campaign is particularly useful in the beginning when your website does not rank on Google, as you can immediately get to the top of search results for your specified keywords. It can be invaluable for attracting new visitors to your website and converting leads into sales.

How can you target your ads?

You can set your ads to appear when someone searches for words or phrases relevant to your business by bidding on certain keywords. You can also target specific locations by setting a mile radius from your business. You can even set your ads to appear at certain times of the day and days of the week and on certain devices more than others. With the power of Adwords, you can narrow down your target audience more than on any other platform.

What makes Adwords ideal for your business?

The biggest advantage to using Adwords over any other form of advertising is that you can be front and center right in the place your customers are looking for you most. These days, when people need something, the first thing they do is search for it on the internet. You'll get a scoop of this action by being at the right place at the right time. Even better, is that you can control your costs since you only pay when someone actually clicks on your ad.
