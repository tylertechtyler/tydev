Developing your website is the next step in the process to creating your dream website

Step 1 Defining technologies Depending on the scope of your web project, we will determine what technology stack will work best for developing, deploying, and managing your site. Most of my sites are developed using HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and Tailwind CSS as a front end framework. Wordpress, Joomla, Gatsby, Weebly, or Shopify will then be selected for content management depending on your management goals.
Step 2 Developing for mobile first Mobile devices account for more than half of all internet traffic, so you should expect most of your visitors will be viewing your site on their phone or tablet. Your site will be developed with a mobile-first design methodology. This means that it will look great no matter what device it is viewed on.
Step 3 Making cross-browser compatible Your site will be developed with compatibility in mind. Due to how diverse the internet is these days, it is extremely important to keep different web browsers in mind. Your site will be built with code that validates properly and will be tested across multiple browsers to ensure compatibility.
Step 4 Making accessible Making your site accessible for people with disabilities makes sense for any business. We'll make sure your site is built with the proper tags, layout, and color schemes so the visually impaired and those relying on screen readers are able to fully access your site.
Step 5 Testing for speed and performance It does not matter how pretty or complex your site is if it isn't fast. An industry study has shown that most visitors will leave your site if it doesn't load within 3 seconds! You don't have to worry about any of this. Your site will be optimized for performance and be as fast as possible.
