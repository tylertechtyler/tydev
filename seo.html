Make sure your website is found by search engines

Search engine optimization is the process of creating your website to be attractive to search engines so you rank highly and show up when customers are looking for you.

Keyword research

It is important to understand what search terms your customers are looking up when they are searching for businesses like yours. These are keywords. Once we know what keywords are most popular for your business, I can make sure your site is built to take full advantage of them.

Fast responsive website

Making your website load fast and be responsive across multiple devices is half the battle of search engine optimization. Sites such as Google will prioritize websites that load fast and are compatible with many types of devices. This is all taken care of for you during the construction of your website.

High quality, fresh content

Search engines prioritize websites that have a lot of high quality, relevant content. As long as your site is packed with good information, you will get favorable results. Another important element is making sure your content is stays fresh and updated. This is all handled for you with my website management service.

Site architecture and proper tags

Submitting a site map of your website to search engines ensures that your pages are indexed properly and quickly to accelerate your ranking. Creating the proper meta tags and title tags for each one of your pages and making sure images have the correct alt tags is also very important to your site showing up properly in search results.
