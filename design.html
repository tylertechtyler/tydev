My web design process is the first step in building your dream website

Step 1 Defining your goals We'll set up a meeting to discuss what you plan to achieve with your website. Who are your primary customers? and are you trying to inform, sell, or amuse? Your website will be built around achieving your goals, so it is important we have a clear idea of what those are before getting started.
Step 2 Competitor analysis Here we have a look at what your competitors are doing and figure out what a website for your industry typically looks like, and the message it conveys. We want to make sure your website not only stands up to the competition, but outshines it completely. We'll also look at some of your favorite websites to get an idea of what your ideal style is.
Step 3 Defining project scope We will figure out the scope of your project by determining how many pages will be created, and what content will be needed on your part such as images and information about your business. We will then use this to create a site map and layout the general architecture of your site.
Step 4 Visual Design Finally, we will create a mockup or wireframe of your site taking into consideration all of your goals, content, and scope. We will find the perfect color scheme and layout of elements for your site. This plan is presented to you for revision before we start the development of your site.
