Deploying your website doesn't have to be complicated

I can handle every step of deploying your website so you don't have to worry about a thing.

Step 1 Determing your needs The first step is to figure out where you are in the process. Do you already have an existing site you are transferring? Do you have hosting already set up, and do you already have a domain name or will we need find one and register it for you? No matter where you are in the process, it is easy to take care of everything to get you going quick.
Step 2 Registering your domain name We'll go over possible domain names together and figure out what works best for you out of what is available. Sometimes the ideal name is already taken and we need to brainstorm the best option, and sometimes it is easy to find the perfect name. Once we have a name, I take care of buying and registering it for you.
Step 3 Setting up hosting If you already have hosting, we can login to your current hosting control panel to configure everything for your new site. If you are getting hosting through me, I will have everything taken care of and ready to go to start hosting your new site. It is easiest if you are starting with nothing, but no matter what your hosting set up looks like, I can handle everything from start to finish.
Step 4 Setting up DNS records DNS stands for domain name system and means the system of information that contains the IP addresses, domain names, hosting, and other registration information across all of the websites on the internet. I will handle everything involved in setting up your DNS records so your website can go live on the web.
Step 5 Testing and backup Before we go live, everything is tested to make sure it works and backed up. If you are moving from an old host, pointing name servers to the new host can take a few days, and needs to be monitored to make sure no problems occur. Once everything is tested and working, we are good to go for deploying your website and you are in business!
